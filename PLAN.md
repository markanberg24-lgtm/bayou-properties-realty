# Bayou Properties Realty — Luxury Real Estate App



## Features

- **Property Listings** — Browse real listings with photos, prices, beds/baths, square footage, and addresses
- **Property Details** — Tap any listing to see full details, photo gallery, description, and a contact button
- **About Us** — Company overview and John Braun's bio with credentials and experience
- **Services** — Overview of residential sales, commercial properties, rentals, and property management
- **Contact** — One-tap call, email, and directions to the Houston office
- **Favorites** — Save properties you're interested in for quick access later

## Design

- **Dark navy background** with rich **gold accents** — a premium, luxury real estate aesthetic
- Inspired by high-end property apps like Sotheby's and Compass
- Large hero property images with elegant card layouts and subtle shadows
- Gold-toned buttons, icons, and section dividers against deep navy surfaces
- Clean typography with generous spacing for a refined mobile feel
- Smooth micro-interactions on cards and buttons

## Screens

- **Home** — Hero banner with "Houston's Choice in Real Estate" tagline, featured listings carousel, quick-access service cards, and a call-to-action
- **Properties** — Grid/list of all real property listings (1607 Chapman St, 5011 Reed Rd, 514 E Bayshore Dr, etc.) with photos, price, and key details
- **Property Detail** — Full-screen photos, price, address, beds/baths/sqft, description, map placeholder, and "Contact Agent" button
- **About** — Company story, John Braun's bio and achievements (HAR Rising Star, Five Star Professional), and areas served
- **Contact** — Phone (281-286-6500), email, office address with tap-to-call and tap-to-email actions

## App Icon

- Dark navy circle with a gold bayou/water-inspired house silhouette and "BP" monogram — elegant and recognizable
